List of Martin's Fish

Salmon

  • The average length of a salmon is 28 in to 30 in.
  • These salmon were harvested in Washington and Maine.
  • They commonly eat Shrimp, Mayflies, Sand eels, and Herring.

    • Catfish

  • The average length of a catfish is 22 inches.
  • These ones were harvested in Alabama and Mississippi!
  • Catfish commonly eat algae, small fish, and scavenged debris.

    • Bass

  • Bass are usually sized around 15.7 inches.
  • They are found in North Carolina and Florida, as well as Mississippi.
  • They like to eat leeches, insects, baitfish, and frogs.

    • Trout

  • Trouts are around 16 inches long, usually.
  • They are usually found in the western half of the US.
  • They like to eat aquatic nisects and other fish.
