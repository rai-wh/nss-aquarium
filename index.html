Locations Martin travelled to

Martin travelled to Mississippi and was able to find not only Catfish but also Trout!

Martin also visited Alabama and found that their were various fish to experience.

Martin's favorite place to visit, though, was California, in which he found a plethora of various fish.

  • The fish tank should be filled with 1 tbsp of salt per 3 gallons of water.

  • You should aim to change your tank's water every two to three weeks.

  • You should also aim to change the tank's gravel once a month

  • Make sure not to overfeed the fish!

  • The optimal temperature of a fish tank should be around 75-80 degrees Fahrenheit.
